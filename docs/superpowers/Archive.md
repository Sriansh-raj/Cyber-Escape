### Houthi Decision-Making on Iran War

> [!quote]
> During the first month of the US-Israel war on Iran, the Houthis adopted a cautious approach, even though many expected them to move faster based on the nature of their close relationship with Tehran.

Saved: 2026-04-07

Source: [Al Jazeera Opinions](https://www.aljazeera.com/opinions/2026/4/7/what-is-driving-the-houthis-decision-making-on-joining-the-iran-war)

#### Key Points

The article examines the Houthi movement's strategic decision-making during the US-Israel war on Iran:

1. **Historical Context**
   - After Operation Al-Aqsa Flood (October 7, 2023), the Houthis launched military action in support of Gaza
   - US and Israel retaliatory strikes in March 2025 lasted two months
   - Oman-brokered agreement in May 2025 brought fighting to a halt

2. **Internal Debate**
   - Some Houthi leaders believe the cost of involvement was high
   - Two clear currents have emerged within the group:
     - **Caution camp**: Pushes for avoiding open confrontation, preserving understandings with Saudi Arabia
     - **Active camp**: Believes present moment is crucial for the "axis of resistance"

3. **Current Stance**
   - Neither full-scale engagement nor total absence
   - Escalation of political rhetoric during first month
   - Limited, carefully calculated operations beginning March 27
   - Gradual intervention declaration

4. **Future Considerations**
   - Pressure may intensify as war escalates regionally
   - "Unity of fronts" talk with Iran intensifies
   - The longer the conflict, the harder to remain in "grey zone"

> [!warning] Key Question
> The question is no longer whether the Houthis will enter the war, but how they will enter and at what cost.

### Wuthering Waves: Aemeath Build & Guide

> [!success] Character Overview
> **Aemeath** is a 5★ rarity character from the Fusion element who uses the Sword type weapon.

Saved: 2026-04-06

Source: [Prydwen Institute](https://www.prydwen.gg/wuthering-waves/characters/aemeath)

#### Character Summary

- **Element**: Fusion
- **Weapon**: Sword
- **Rarity**: 5★
- **Role**: Main DPS
- **Patch**: 3.1

> [!tip] Tier Rating
> - **DPS**: T0
> - **Tower of Adversity**: T1.5
> - **Whimpering Wastes**: T1

#### Pros

- The strongest Fusion DPS in the game
- Multiple modes of operation (Tune Rupture, Fusion Burst, Mono Fusion)
- Works well without Signature weapon
- High team flexibility

#### Cons

- Requires premium teammates for optimal performance
- Forte generation depends on melee Basic Attacks
- Fusion Burst mode needs strong secondary teammate

#### Skills Overview

**Basic Attack - Aemeath**
: Perform up to **4** consecutive attacks, dealing Fusion DMG.

**Heavy Attack - Aemeath**
: Hold Normal Attack to charge, dealing Fusion DMG.
: Two charging levels available.

**Form Switch**
: Aemeath can switch between herself and her Mech form.
: The Mech inherits stats and unlocks new moves.

**Heavenfall Edict - Overdrive**
: Deals Fusion DMG and switches to Mech form.
: Grants Stardust Resonance for **30s**.
: Unlocks Heavenfall Edict: Unbound for **60s**.

**Seraphic Duo**
: Entered when casting Basic Attack Stage 4.
: Enables powerful Forte Skills.

**Resonance Skill - Seraphic Duet**
: Requires 100 Synchronization Rate.
: Two variants: Overture (Aemeath form) and Encore (Mech form).

> [!info] Resonance Mode
> Aemeath has two Resonance Modes:
> - **Tune Rupture**: For single-target scenarios
> - **Fusion Burst**: For AoE scenarios

**Resonance Mode - Tune Rupture**
: When Resonators inflict Tune Rupture - Interfered, inflict **10** stacks of Rupturous Trail for **30s**.
: Tune Rupture Response - Starburst deals damage to affected targets.

**Resonance Mode - Fusion Burst**
: When Resonators inflict Fusion Burst, inflict **1** stack of Fusion Trail for **30s**.
: Triggers Fusion Burst based on max stack limit.
